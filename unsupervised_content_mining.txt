## Version Writing
The model is not the system

Version 1 — Simple
Most people think building a good ML model solves the problem.

It doesn’t.

The model is just one part of the system.

You still need:

a decision layer
an action layer
a feedback loop

Without these, your model is just producing numbers.

Version 2 — Contrarian

Everyone is obsessed with improving model accuracy.

But accuracy is not your problem.

Your real problem is this:

You don’t have a system.

You have:

a model
no decision rules
no actions
no feedback

So even a perfect model gives you zero business value.

Version 3 — Story-Based

A team builds a churn model with 92% accuracy.

They celebrate.

But nothing changes.

Why?

Because no one decided:

what score triggers intervention
what action to take
how to measure success

Six months later, churn is the same.

The model worked.

The system didn’t.
